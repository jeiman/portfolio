Side Note

My goal was and still is pretty simple — to change the world. It all started back in my college days when my first computer science module for the year involved me designing a simple HTML webpage for an assignment. From then on I realised that there's a lot of potential in creating content using the web technologies that were available at the time and more to come in the future (NodeJS, AngularJS that are available now). Hence, I decided to pursue my passion in the field of web development. I founded up my very own freelance company, Techsols Enterprise in which I provide web and IT solutions to clients in need of my service. I have designed several websites for clients since then.

I get this zest-feeling when I start coding, knowing that I could turn a simple product into a marketable content for consumers to use. Furthermore, once you finish a project and feel great about it, you know that you can achieve more than you've done in the past. All of which by utilising the web technologies that are currently in the market.
